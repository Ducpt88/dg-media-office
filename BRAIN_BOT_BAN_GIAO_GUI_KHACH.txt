4. Setup qua Telegram

Co the gui truc tiep link vao bot Telegram.

Vi du:

https://www.facebook.com/yourpage
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=abc

Bot se tu nhan dien day la nguon moi va them vao he thong.

Neu muon setup day du, gui mau nay:

SETUP_BRAIN
TELEGRAM_BOT_TOKEN=PASTE_TOKEN_HERE
TELEGRAM_CHAT_ID=PASTE_CHAT_ID_HERE
SOURCE=https://www.facebook.com/yourpage
SOURCE=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=abc
INTERVAL=20
LIMIT=50
RUN_ONCE=yes
START_WATCH=yes
CLEANUP=yes


5. Y nghia cac dong lenh Telegram

SETUP_BRAIN
Bao cho bot biet day la lenh setup day du.

TELEGRAM_BOT_TOKEN
Token cua bot Telegram, lay tu BotFather.

TELEGRAM_CHAT_ID
Noi nhan bao cao: ca nhan, group hoac channel.

SOURCE
Nguon can quet. Co the co nhieu dong SOURCE.

INTERVAL
Bao nhieu phut quet lai mot lan.

LIMIT
Moi lan quet toi da bao nhieu link.

RUN_ONCE
yes = setup xong chay thu mot vong.

START_WATCH
yes = setup xong bat che do chay lien tuc.

CLEANUP
yes = xoa audio/video nang sau khi tao xong tai lieu.
